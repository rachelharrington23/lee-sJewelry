Shipping and Packaging

Materials

Shipping out necklaces requires a variety of materials to ensure your necklice arrives safe. In my orders I use a clear jewelry bag, a bubble mailer, gorilla tape, and a stamp to finish it off.

Processing Time

The time it takes me to package an order varies based on school, afterschool activities, and overall motivation. Overall processing time usually takes one to four days.

Shipping Time

Shipping time normally depends on the distance it takes from me to where the customer lives. An average time for shipping is two to ten days.

Cost

Although it costs me to pay for shipping materials the Shipping price is free for all customers with every necklace purchase!! Occasionally there will also be coupons and discounts!!
